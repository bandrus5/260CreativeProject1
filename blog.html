It Sucks to be a Book

April 3rd, 2017

You know what really sucks? There are so many books in the world, no one can read all of them. That sucks for people. It sucks for books, too. Even the most successful book will only be read by a fraction of the literate population of the Earth. Unless it's the Bible. Or Harry Potter. For the rest of them, they are up a creek.
