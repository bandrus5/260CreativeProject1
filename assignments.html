Assignments

Below is a list of assignments that you will be required to complete during the course.

Each assignment should be turned in at the beginning of class on the due date listed, unless otherwise specified.

Book Talk

Due: March 21st

For this assignment, you will need to pick a favorite book from outside of class and give a report on it to your classmates.

Books can be from any genre, and your report should be 3-5 minutes long.


Theme Notebook

Due: March 29th

Find a current event, relavent article, or other external source that relates to what we have been reading in class. Write a short summary of your experience.
